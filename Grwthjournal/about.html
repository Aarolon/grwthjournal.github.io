About Us

About Grwth

Grwth was originally founded from the struggles of a close friend. They were facing a tough time in their life, affected severely by depression and anxiety. What got them out of this, was journaling. But it took time and a strong desire to overcome their struggle. This made them believe there could be a better solution to what their original problem was, knowing how to journal in a way that most positively impacted their mental health while also keeping them engaged. This is where Grwth comes in. By using machine learning models with professional counseling and therapy based analyses to create structured prompts relative to a user’s needs, the guidance our friend desired to both keep them engaged and to support their mental health improvement was finally real. Mental health affects us all, and pressures such as high workload or balancing roles and relationships can really put a strain on our mental health. This can lead to many prevalent mental health problems
